Osamu Dazai

Osamu Dazai is a member of the Armed Detective Agency and former executive of the underworld organization,
Port Mafia. He has the Ability named No Longer Human.

Atsushi Nakajima

Atsushi Nakajima is a member of the Armed Detective Agency who possesses the ability Beast Beneath the Moonlight.

Ranpo Edogawa

Ranpo Edogawa is a member of the Armed Detective Agency. Despite not being an ability user, he is known as one of the best detectives highly sought by the police, renowned by his comrades, himself, and others as the "greatest detective in the world".

Doppo Kunikida

Doppo Kunikida is a member of the Armed Detective Agency. He owns the ability named Matchless Poet.

Kyoka Izumi

Kyoka Izumi is the newest member of the Armed Detective Agency after defecting from the Port Mafia who has the ability Demon Snow.

Yukichi Fukuzawa

Yukichi Fukuzawa is the president of the Armed Detective Agency. He has the Ability named All Men Are Equal.

Akiko Yosano

Akiko Yosano is a member and personal physician of the Armed Detective Agency. Her ability is a rare healing ability, Thou Shalt Not Die.

Junichiro Tanizaki

Junichiro Tanizaki is a member of the Armed Detective Agency and the older brother of Naomi Tanizaki who owns the ability named Light Snow.

Kenji Miyazawa

Kenji Miyazawa is a member of the Armed Detective Agency. He has the ability Undefeated by the Rain.
