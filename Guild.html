Francis Fitzgerald

Francis Scott Key Fitzgerald is the latest leader of the American organization known as the Guild until its eventual dissolution and split, upon which he currently leads the Renewed Guild. His ability name is known as The Great Fitzgerald.

Louisa Alcott

Louisa May Alcott is a former member of the Guild, after which she joins the Renewed Guild after the organization's dissolution and split. She wields the ability Little Women.

Edgar Allan Poe

Edgar Allan Poe is the former master architect of the Guild, and his ability is Black Cat in the Rue Morgue.

Howard Phillips Lovecraft

Howard Phillips Lovecraft is a former member of the Guild. He has the Ability named Great Old One.

John Steinbeck

John Ernst Steinbeck is a former member of the Guild. Since the dissolution of the Guild after the war, he temporarily leads the Surviving Guild Forces. His ability is named The Grapes of Wrath.

Lucy Maud Montgomery

Lucy Maud Montgomery is a former member of the American organization known as the Guild. Her ability is known as Anne of Abyssal Red.

Margaret Mitchell

Margaret Mitchell is a former member and apprentice of the American organization known as the Guild. Her ability is known as Gone With the Wind.

Nathaniel Hawthorne

Nathaniel Hawthorne is a former member of the organization known as the Guild and later affiliated with the Rats in the House of the Dead. His ability is known as The Scarlet Letter.

Mark Twain

Mark Twain is a former member of the Guild, and his ability is Huck Finn and Tom Sawyer.
