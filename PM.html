Ryunosuke Akutagawa

Ryunosuke Akutagawa is a member of the Port Mafia and has the ability Rashomon.

Ogai Mori

Ogai Mori is the leader of the Port Mafia. He has the ability named Vita Sexualis.

Chuya Nakahara

Chuya Nakahara is a member of the Port Mafia as well as Dazai's former partner in Twin Dark. He is one of the five Executives in the Port Mafia. He has the Ability named Upon the Tainted Sorrow.

Osamu Dazai

Osamu Dazai is a member of the Armed Detective Agency and former executive of the underworld organization,
Port Mafia. He has the Ability named No Longer Human.

Koyo Ozaki

Koyo Ozaki is a Port Mafia executive wielding the ability Golden Demon.

Ichiyo Higuchi

Ichiyo Higuchi is a mafioso of the Port Mafia who works under Ryunosuke Akutagawa's orders and acts as his assistant and bodyguard.

Kyusaku Yumeno

Kyusaku Yumeno is a member of the Port Mafia. Their ability is Dogra Magra.

Sakunosuke Oda

Sakunosuke Oda was a member of the Port Mafia. His ability name was Flawless.

Ango Sakaguchi

Ango Sakaguchi is a government worker from the Special Division for Unusual Powers whose ability is Discourse on Decadence. He was close friends with Sakunosuke Oda and Osamu Dazai four years ago.

Kyoka Izumi

Kyoka Izumi is the newest member of the Armed Detective Agency after defecting from the Port Mafia who has the ability Demon Snow.
